Australian energy accounts dashboard

Power BI

How is Australia's energy consumption distributed — and is the country actually transitioning away from fossil fuels? Built on ABS data covering 7 industries and 9 energy products from 2010–2023.

  • Key finding: fossil fuels still account for 57.11% of total energy — Natural gas alone at 57,668 PJ dominates all other products
  • Mining is the largest consuming industry at 51,705 PJ (40.92%), followed by Electricity/gas/water at 29,890 PJ (23.66%)
  • Fossil fuel average usage has declined from ~400 PJ/year in 2010 toward ~270 PJ by 2023 — non-fossil sources rising over same period
  • Power Query: unpivoted 14 year columns, replaced ABS suppressed values (np → null), removed footnote markers from product names, split year delimiter
  • DAX: Fossil Fuel Flag calculated column, % of Total Energy measure, Top Industry and Top Product cards

Retail store sales dashboard

Power BI

Which product categories drive the most revenue, and how does performance vary across months and payment methods? Built end-to-end from a deliberately messy raw dataset requiring significant cleaning before analysis.

  • KPIs: $1,552,071 total revenue · 11,971 transactions · 66,276 units sold · $129.65 avg order value
  • Top category by revenue: Butchers ($208K), closely followed by Electric Household Essentials ($204K) and Beverages ($197K)
  • Payment method split nearly even: Cash 34.27%, Digital Wallet 32.92%, Credit Card 32.80%
  • Revenue peaks in January ($160K) then dips significantly through mid-year — seasonal pattern visible on monthly trend line
  • Power Query cleaning: removed duplicate Transaction IDs, built derived Price Per Unit column for nulls using Total Spent ÷ Quantity, capitalised categories, trimmed all columns, filtered incomplete rows
  • Date Table via CALENDAR() DAX with Month Name sorted by Month Number to fix x-axis ordering

AFL games Excel dashboard

Excel

Which teams win the most, which venues draw the biggest crowds, and how did COVID-19 impact AFL attendance? Analysed 2,879 games across 2012–2025 using Excel pivot tables, formulas, and slicers.

  • KPIs: 2,879 total games · 32,430 average attendance · 100,024 highest recorded attendance
  • MCG is the dominant venue at 52,661 avg attendance — nearly 25% higher than the next largest (Perth Stadium at 41,297)
  • COVID-19 impact clearly visible: attendance collapsed from 36,317 in 2019 to 6,377 in 2020, recovering to 38,227 by 2025
  • Geelong leads all-time wins (223), ahead of Sydney (207) and Hawthorn (192) — Grand Final rounds spike attendance to ~90,000+
  • Winner column via nested IF pulling dynamic team names; Attendance tier (Low/Med/High) via nested IF on 30K/50K thresholds
  • Custom round sort order applied to fix alphabetical pivot ordering; GetPivotData disabled for clean KPI card references
